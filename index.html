Welcome to The Salvation Army College

A center of academic excellence and spiritual growth

Building Future Leaders

Founded on the principles of academic excellence and Christian values, The Salvation Army College, Ibadan has been a beacon of quality education since its establishment. We are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, character, and spiritual values.

Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities create an environment where students can thrive academically, socially, and spiritually. We believe in holistic education that prepares students not just for examinations, but for life.
